About Us

We are dedicated to making camping tents more sustainable by repairing and recycling old tents. Our initiative helps reduce waste and supports the local economy by creating jobs.

Our Services

Repair Services

We offer professional repair services for all types of camping tents. Extend the life of your tent and keep it out of the landfill.

Recycling Program

Bring us your old tents, and we'll recycle the materials to create new products. Help us reduce waste and promote sustainability.

Educational Workshops

Join our workshops to learn how to repair your own tents and other gear. Empowering you with the skills to be more sustainable.
